The real ability bar — what actually gets you in真正的能力门槛 —— 到底凭什么录取

This is the part most guides get wrong. You asked about things like "does HSMC need a PSAT score, and how high?" Here is the honest, program-by-program answer about the abilities and numbers that actually decide admission — separate from the paperwork.这是大多数攻略写错的部分。你问的是诸如"HSMC 是不是要 PSAT、要多少分？"这类问题。下面是逐项目、关于真正决定录取的能力与硬指标的诚实回答 —— 与"交什么材料"是两回事。

First, the big myth to clear up先破除一个最大的误区

None of these 9 elite programs admit by SAT/PSAT score. SAT/PSAT measures grade-level math; these programs select for mathematical depth far beyond it. The HSMC "PSAT" idea is a confusion with a different kind of program (e.g. CTY/Duke TIP talent searches, which use SAT/ACT as an above-level filter). For the MIT list, the real currency is: their own admission test, a proof-based problem set, or contest results (AMC/AIME) — not a standardized score.这 9 个顶尖项目没有一个是看 SAT/PSAT 分数录取的。SAT/PSAT 衡量的是同年级数学水平；而这些项目筛选的是远超于此的数学深度。所谓"HSMC 要 PSAT"是和另一类项目（如 CTY / Duke TIP 这类天才选拔，用 SAT/ACT 做"超前测试"筛选）搞混了。对 MIT 这份清单，真正的"硬通货"是：项目自己的入学测试、证明式问题集，或竞赛成绩（AMC/AIME）—— 不是标化分数。

Age: 12–18
Length: 3 weeks
Fit★★★★★
